# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes demonstrating empathy, respecting differing viewpoints, giving and accepting constructive feedback, accepting responsibility, and focusing on community benefit.

Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language or attention, trolling or personal attacks, harassment, publishing private information without permission, and conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders clarify and enforce acceptable behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions and communicate moderation reasons when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code applies within project spaces and when an individual officially represents the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior privately to repository maintainers. Reports receive prompt, fair review. Maintainers protect reporter privacy and follow the Contributor Covenant enforcement guidelines when choosing corrective action.
